Standard booking estimates

The standard booking phase will open later this year, closing on the 31st September. During the standard booking phase you will be able to book for IST, CMT, as well as additional participants beyond your early bird booking.

We are asking for a prediction of what you are likely to request in September. This is not a commitment to make a standard booking.

Contingent Management Team

We recommend that members of Contingent Management Teams are aged 27 years old or above as the CMT responsibilities will likely distract from their participation in the Moot. However, we recognise some contingents may need to have CMT members aged 18 to 26 years. If members of a CMT are of participant age, they will be subject to the higher participant fees and should be counted within the participant numbers for the purpose of booking.

International Service Team

Individuals aged 27 years and above on the first day of the Moot (18th July 2022) can attend as members of the International Service Team. In effect this means that IST must have been born on or before the 18th July 1995.
