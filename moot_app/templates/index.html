Early Bird Booking

Welcome to the Early Bird Booking portal. The following form will allow you to make an Early Bird Participant Booking.

Early Bird Bookings will be accepted until the 30th April 2021. You may increase or decrease your Early Bird Commitment by 10% without penalty before the 31st July 2021. Payment for your Early Bird commitment is due by the 1st September 2021. You can find the complete registration and fee schedule in Bulletin 1.

Any questions about this form or registration should be directed to contingentsupport@moot2021.ie

Country Fee Group Early Bird Participant Fee
A € 225
B € 450
C € 675
D € 900

Only National Scout Organisations (NSO) with WOSM membership may register a Contingent, and there can only be one Contingent per NSO.

You should only complete this form if you are empowered to represent your NSO.

By filling out this form, you are making a financial commitment on behalf of your organisation.
