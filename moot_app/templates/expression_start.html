Expression of Intrest

The following form will allow you to submit an expression of intrest for standard booking of participants, IST and CMT for the 16th World Scout Moot.

Standard bookings will open in September 2021. This form is to provide the Moot team with your best estimate of the number of places you are likley to book in September.

Any questions about this form or more generally about preparing a contingent to the 16th World Scout Moot should be directed to contingentsupport@moot2021.ie

Standard Fees

Country Fee Group Participants IST & CMT

Only National Scout Organisations (NSO) with WOSM membership may register a Contingent, and there can only be one Contingent per NSO.

You should only complete this form if you are empowered to represent your NSO.
